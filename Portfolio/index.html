About Me

Hello, my name is Andrew Wynn. I live in Atlanta, Georgia and am twenty-two years old.I have a BBA in Information Security and Assurance and an aspiring entrepreneur. I am an avid coffee drinker,DJ, Storyteller and enjoy long walks to the fridge.

One thing I am passionate about is telling people's stories. In life there a many situations that can be approached form multiple perspectives. I believe in telling other people's stories that I have the ability to changes someones outlook in life.

Past Experiences

I have been in multiple lines of work. From working in horticulture, to being a Resident Assistant at Kennesaw State University,Real Estate,being a DJ, from running a media agency to being a Systems intern.

How have these experiences shaped me? All these professions are different, but they center around three of my passions people, creativity and problem solving. I am passionate about people, who they are and what they have to say. I believe that every person matters and have something important to say. I enjoy creating. From figuring how to design a space to figuring out what I can sell to stay alive. Lastly I enjoy problem solving. If there is a problem I want to fix it. I believe there is a solution to every problem in the world, we just need to address it from different perspectives. I always believe to stay curious because drive and ambition with every opportunity could change your life.
